    First Time Fix It was established in 2017 by veteran and mechanic John Garza.

    Our mechanics have more than 30 years of combined experience. We believe in providing high-quality repairs with high-quality parts at a reasonable price.

    And, just to back you up, we provide a 12-month/12-000-mile warranty with all of our repairs.

    We ensure the proper time is taken with every job to make sure each vehicle is accurately diagnosed and repaired without any surprises or comebacks.

    At First Time Fix It, we fix it the first time.
